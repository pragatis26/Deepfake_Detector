How It Works

01

Face Detection

MTCNN detects and crops the face region from your media for focused analysis.

02

Deep Feature Extraction

EfficientNet-B4 (pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on FF++) extracts spatial and frequency artifacts.

03

Binary Classification

A sigmoid output layer gives the probability of the media being fake vs real.

04

Grad-CAM Explainability

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Maps highlight exactly which facial regions triggered the decision.
